Plot

NASCAR racer Ricky Bobby becomes the best racer in the US. After some antics with his friend Cal Naughton Jr. a foriegn racer Jean Girad comes from France to challenge Ricky Bobby to a race. Ricky Bobby initially gets into a crash is unable to race. After some help from his friends he is able to race again and then beat his rival Jean Girad.
